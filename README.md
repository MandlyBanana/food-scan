# Food scan #
## What is food scan? ##
Food scan is an app designed to help you and your housemates to reduce and make sure you know just how much food you are throwing out on a weekly basis. Because worldwide around 30% of produced food gets thrown out when it could have been eaten. And 18% of this waste comes from households. Therefore this app can change how you look at your food and your view at waste.

## Why we developed food svan ##
We a lot of unnescescarry waste in our daily life and wanted to reduce that in some way. Therefore we developed this prototype of an app that might help just a little bit.
